blaxTARLINES @ Biennale Arte 2026 by Koyo Kouoh
blaxxMEDIA (Livestream and Pre-Recorded Broadcasts)
Consistent with our duplex exhibition model, events in host cities such as Kumasi and other blaxTARLINES "stations" in Africa and around the world will be broadcast to Venice serving as a viewing centre for blaxxMEDIA programming off-Venice, while looping pre-recorded videos of events staged by blaxTARLINES members and affiliates. Exhibitions, Workshops, Art Talks, concerts, listening sessions, reading sessions, poetry, meetings, and more will be planned, programmed, and livestreamed. This component is also progressive and will run throughout the period of Biennale Arte (May 9 - November 22, 2026).
